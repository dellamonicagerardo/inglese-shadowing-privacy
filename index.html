# Privacy Policy – Inglese Shadowing **Last updated:** 25 June 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application **Inglese Shadowing** (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects user information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. ## 1. Information We Collect The App is designed to minimize data collection. ### Microphone and Voice Data The App may request access to your device microphone to enable pronunciation and speaking practice features. Voice data is used only to compare pronunciation during learning sessions. Audio processing is performed locally on the device or through the device’s built-in speech recognition services (such as Google or Samsung speech services). We do **not** store, record, or upload your voice to our own servers. ## 2. Notifications The App may send local notifications to remind users to complete daily exercises or maintain learning streaks. Notifications are generated locally on the device. No notification-related data is transmitted to our servers. ## 3. Local Database (SQLite) The App uses a local SQLite database to store: * learning progress * completed exercises * daily streaks * saved phrases and lessons This information remains stored only on your device. We do **not** collect or transmit this data to external servers. ## 4. Account and Personal Data Currently, the App does **not** require account registration. We do **not** collect: * email addresses * names * phone numbers * passwords * payment information ## 5. Internet Usage The App may access the internet only when necessary for system-level services such as: * text-to-speech (TTS) * speech recognition services provided by the device manufacturer * downloading optional learning content or updates The App does not use its own backend servers to store personal data. ## 6. Third-Party Services The App may rely on third-party services provided by your device or operating system, such as: * Google Speech Services * Android Text-to-Speech Engine * Samsung Voice Services These services may have their own privacy policies. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of those providers. ## 7. Data Security Because user data is stored locally on the device, security depends partly on the security of your device. We recommend keeping your device protected with: * screen lock * biometric authentication * operating system updates ## 8. Children’s Privacy The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. ## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date. ## 10.
